Doctor Who

The official, and iconic, Doctor Who logo, which represents the famous sci-fi series.

Doctor Who is a british science-fiction telivision programme produced by the BBC since 1963. The programme depicts the adventures of the Doctor, a time-travelling humanoid alien. The Doctor explores the universe in a time-travelling spaceship called the TARDIS, which stands for Time and Relative Dimensions in Space. Its exterior appears as a blue British police box, which was a common sight in Britain when the series first aired.

Accompanied by companions, the Doctor combats a variety of foes, while working to save civilizations and help ordinary people. The show is a significant part of British popular culture, and has gained a huge amount of followers elsewhere.

Doctor Who has been influential in the telivision industry and has spawned numerous spin-offs, including books, audio dramas, and a long-running series of comics.
